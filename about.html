Our Patients Are at the Centre of Everything We Do

‘Suwani Pharmacy’ in Hettipola, Kurunegala, is a locally started, small scale Pharmacy that grew considerably over the course of 25 years and became one of the popular sellers of pharmaceutical items in the area. The owner, Mr. Meegoda has managed to upbring his reputation in the business field, by timely fulfilling Customer Orders, availability of quality services and resources, and maintaining customer intimacy. Creating a pleasant work area for the employees had considerably affected the efficiency , resulting in a growth mindset.

With the expansion of the business, and with the traditional way of manual book-keeping system, it has not been an easy task for Mr. Meegoda to keep track of all the records regarding sales, stock items, suppliers and channeling info etc. So a partially automated system was purchased to tend to the daily operations. But now, the system has certain outdated features. Due to the increasing number of mishandlings, to invest for an upgraded and advanced system for the management of the pharmacy activities was inevitable. This is where our system merge with the pharmacy. This smart decision will clear out a tremendous workload from the owner as well as the employees in daily operations.
